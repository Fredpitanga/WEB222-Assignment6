My name is Fred da Silveira Pitanga Filho, I am currentlty a computer programming student at Seneca Polytechnic and this is my company 6ix Puppies

6ix Puppies is a company based in Toronto with the main goal of connecting people who dream of having puppies with puppies who are waiting for a loving family.

Every dog deserves a home, and every home deserves a puppy. Our mission is to create safe, responsible adoptions that match adorable pups with their perfect companions. Whether you're looking for a playful lab or a cuddly corgi, we're here to help you find your new best friend.

Thank you for supporting ethical puppy adoption and helping us give these little tails a happy home.
